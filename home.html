YOUR TIME. BUS.

This website provides a complete bus timetable for all routes starting from Rajarata University to every district in Sri Lanka. It covers all 25 districts, making it easy for university students to check bus timings in one place. The information is based on official sources and updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

With a fully responsive design, the site is accessible on mobile, tablet, and computer. Users can also search and filter bus times by district, helping them save time and choose the right bus without confusion. This project is developed by Gen-Z as a community service initiative, aiming to create a valuable resource for society.
